How to contact me

Email

If you want to communicate with me asynchronously, this is the easiest and fastest way to do so. You may send me an email at contact@jaylenryan.com. If possible, please encrypt your messages using my public key.

If you have GnuPG installed, you may import my public key by running one of the commands below:

$ gpg --keyserver hkps://keys.openpgp.org --recv-key 9E8F6B3541CF737743A77EDF3A2697CDCE87B735
$ curl -sf https://jaylenryan.com/contact@jaylenryan.com-pub.asc | gpg --import

Matrix

If you prefer a more synchronous means of communication, then you may message me on Matrix at @jcryan:matrix.org.

The Fediverse

Note: Messages sent over the fediverse are NOT end-to-end encrypted, meaning that the instance admin(s) could hypothetically view any private messages in plaintext. For this reason, I recommend against using this option, but if it is necessary or you don't care about the aforementioned issue, you may message me on any of my fediverse accounts listed here under the "Fediverse" section.

Sidenote

If I don't respond to you in a timely manner, and you're not trying to sell me something, I'm most likely not ignoring you. I have a bad habit of opening messages and forgetting to respond to them, so I apologize in advance if this happens to you. In the event this does happen, you may (in a respectful manner) message me again.
